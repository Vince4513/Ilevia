What Are You able to do

There is three main section on our website that will allow you to book, validate your tickets or see the statistics of our network.

Validation

To validate your transport pass, you must select a station and be connected to your account.

Buy a ticket

To buy a ticket, you don't need to be log in your account. To do so you need to click on the "BUY A TICKET" button in the navigation bar.

Statistics

The statistics inform you in real time about everything in our company. Do not hesitate to go and have a look at them.
